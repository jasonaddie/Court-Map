An Interactive map of judicial decision publications by the Kyrgyz Republic courts

According to the Access to Information Law, all courts have to publish their decisions on the official site www.act.sot.kg. This site is open to the public so that it will make the judicial process more transparent.

65 out of 73 Kyrgyzstan courts currently use this website resulting in 50% of all decisions being published online.

Use the interactive map and the table to view the number of publications by the case types and court level. You will be able to see how the publications have been increasing since 2015.
